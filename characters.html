Characters

Character Description
Mia Dolan (Emma Stone) An aspiring actress who works as a barista while attending countless auditions.
Sebastian Wilder (Ryan Gosling) A jazz musician with dreams of opening his own club.
Keith (John Legend) A successful musician who tempts Sebastian to join his pop-jazz band.
Laura (Rosemarie DeWitt) Sebastian’s practical sister, worried about his future.
David (Finn Wittrock) Mia’s stable but unexciting boyfriend at the start of the film.
Greg Earnest (Jason Fuchs) An actor who goes on a double date with Mia, symbolizing her routine life.
